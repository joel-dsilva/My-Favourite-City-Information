Dal Baati Churma

This iconic Rajasthani dish pairs baked wheat balls (baati) with hearty lentil curry (dal) and sweet, crumbled wheat (churma). Originally created as a warrior's food, it was designed to stay fresh during long desert journeys‚Äîmaking it as practical as it is delicious.
